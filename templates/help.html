Need Help? You're in the right place!

Pulsivity is a web app where you can store your blood pressure readings, view them graphically, receive medically-grounded insights, and export your data. Let's get started!

Upon entry to your home page, there are 3 boxes for input: Systolic, Diastolic, and Pulse.

Assuming that you have a blood pressure machine (otherwise referred to as a sphygmomanometer) to take your blood pressure readings, Systolic is the numerator, Diastolic is the denominator, and Pulse is a separate metric.

Enter those readings, respectively, and click 'Add Entry'.

Your entry will then be added to the table below, with the date and time of entry.

You can also see a chart of your blood pressure readings over time, with the option to toggle between systolic and diastolic.

From there, you can also toggle notifications to be emailed (this feature still need to be refined).

That's all you need to know for now! Watch the video for a more detailed walkthrough!
